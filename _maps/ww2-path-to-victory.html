A new World War II map, inspired by Global 1940, from the creators of G40 Balanced Mod.

Features a newly redrawn Russia, China, and Pacific theater, designed to encourage more dynamic and historical gameplay.

Also includes new unit mechanics, such as carriers that scramble, factories that are destroyed upon capture, and combined-arms support for mechs and tanks.
