Zombies-World War 2 is inspired by the game Axis & Allies & Zombies. There are 2 scenarios.
* Zombies-World War 2 has Zombies randomly popping up around the world, an enemy both to the Axis & the Allies
* World War II v7 removes the zombies, for a simple, fast-play game
