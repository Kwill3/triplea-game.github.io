3 Variants on the New World Order Map that handle smaller neutral nations differently
New World Order Variant - Neutrals Unassigned
Neutral nations are restricted to their homeland and can be attacked by either alliance
New World Order Variant - Neutrals Assigned
Neutral nations are restricted to their homeland and are assigned to an alliance
New World Order Variant - Neutrals Unrestricted
Neutral nations are normal members of an alliance
