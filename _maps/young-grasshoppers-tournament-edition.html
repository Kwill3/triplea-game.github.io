Young Grasshoppers Tournament Edition is based on the victory objective system developed by Young Grasshopper for tournament play of Axis and Allies 1940 Global 2nd Edition.


Key features include combining UK Pacific and ANZAC into one nation, revised order of play for nations, victory objectives and 8 rounds of play.
